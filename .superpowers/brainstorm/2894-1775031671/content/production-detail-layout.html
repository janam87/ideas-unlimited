Key Design Decisions

  • Hero: Full-bleed video (YouTube embed like home) or photo with gradient overlay. Title at bottom-left.
  • Poster + Synopsis: Poster is position: sticky on left. Synopsis + details scroll on right. On mobile: poster on top, content below.
  • Cast & Crew: All names are gold links → /people/[slug]. Roles on the right in mono.
  • Gallery: Full-width edge-to-edge grid (not constrained to max-w-6xl). Photos + videos mixed. Lightbox on click.
  • Press & Reviews: 2-column grid. Supports press articles, newspaper cutout images, and audience reviews.
